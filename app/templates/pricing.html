Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — the Free plan lets you try the full pipeline with 10 applications. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel any time?

Absolutely. Cancel from settings at any time. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

Never. The bot fills forms and sends you a preview via Telegram. You tap Approve — then and only then does it submit. Full control, always.
